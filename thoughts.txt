
VLM-Based Data Extraction
Uses Ollama VLM API (specifically qwen3-vl:235b-cloud model) to analyze product images
Extracts structured product information including:
Product codes (e.g., "1059-01-04", "1105-43-06")
Technical specifications from tables (ID mm, ID tum, YD mm, working pressure, bend radius, weight)
Product names/categories (e.g., "KAPPAFLEX 2K PO")
Construction details (materials, temperature ratings, etc.)
Bounding boxes for visual verification
